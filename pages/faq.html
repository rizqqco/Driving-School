In New Jersey, students must hold a valid learner's permit before beginning behind-the-wheel instruction. Permits are available at age 16. We recommend scheduling your first lesson shortly after receiving the permit so you can build skills during the required holding period.

Yes — New Jersey law requires a valid learner's permit for all behind-the-wheel driving lessons. You can obtain your permit at any NJ MVC location by passing the written knowledge test. We're happy to point you to resources to help you study for the permit test.

Yes, the 5-Hour Pre-Licensing Course is required by the New Jersey MVC before you can schedule a road test. It covers defensive driving techniques, NJ traffic laws, and safe driving practices. We offer this course regularly — contact us for upcoming dates and enrollment.
