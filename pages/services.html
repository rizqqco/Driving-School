5-Hour Pre-Licensing Course

NJ MVC Required

New Jersey requires all first-time drivers to complete a 5-hour pre-licensing course before their road test. Our course is engaging, comprehensive, and covers defensive driving, NJ traffic laws, and safe driving habits. Certificate issued upon completion.

  • MVC-approved curriculum
  • In-person or online options
  • Official completion certificate
  • Required before road test

Road Test Preparation

Pre-Test Session

A focused 2-hour session where we drive the actual road test route at your MVC location. We cover every maneuver the examiner will evaluate — parallel parking, three-point turn, lane changes, and more. Students who complete this session feel dramatically more confident on test day.

  • Drive the actual test route
  • Examiner scoring criteria review
  • Common mistake correction
  • Test-day tips & strategy
