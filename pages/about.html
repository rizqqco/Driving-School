About American Star

Our Story

American Star Driving School was founded with a simple belief: every student deserves a patient, professional instructor who takes their learning seriously. Too many people arrive at their road test unprepared or feeling anxious — we set out to change that.

Serving communities across New Jersey, our school has helped hundreds of teens and adults earn their licenses and gain the skills to drive safely for a lifetime. We are fully licensed and registered with the New Jersey Motor Vehicle Commission.

Whether you're a nervous teenager picking up the keys for the first time, or an adult who never had the chance to learn, we meet you exactly where you are — and we don't move on until you're confident.

What We Stand For

These values guide every lesson we teach and every student we serve.

Safety First

Every decision we make — from our dual-control vehicles to our curriculum — is rooted in keeping students and everyone on the road safe.

Patience & Encouragement

Learning to drive is stressful. Our instructors are trained to be calm, positive, and encouraging — no matter how many times you need to practice a maneuver.

Accountability

We hold ourselves accountable for your progress. If you're not ready for your road test, we'll tell you honestly — and keep working with you until you are.

Community

We're a local, family-run school — not a franchise. We invest in the communities we serve and take pride in every driver we put on New Jersey roads.
